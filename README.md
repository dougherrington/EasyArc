# EasyArc

**The fastest and easiest way to play retro games on your Mac. Period.**

Ever wanted to just pick up a controller and play your retro game collection without wrestling with RetroArch, Dolphin, PCSX2 and a dozen other emulators? That's exactly what EasyArc does.

Point it at your ROM folder. EasyArc automatically figures out what system each game belongs to, launches the right emulator, configures your controller, and gets out of the way. No command lines. No manual configuration. No technical knowledge required.

![EasyArc Screenshot](screenshot.png)

---

## What is EasyArc?

EasyArc sits on top of the best retro game emulators available and handles everything automatically — controller setup, game detection, game artwork and descriptions and, most importantly, launching! You just pick a game and press play.

---

## Features

- 🎮 **Plug and play** — connect a compatible Bluetooth controller and EasyArc configures it automatically
- 🖼️ **Beautiful box art** — automatically downloads cover art and game metadata for your library
- 🕹️ **Countless games** — the sky — or space — is the limit!
- ✨ **Stunning holographic UI** — this doesn't look like your grandfather's emulator
- 🔍 **Smart game detection** — just point EasyArc at your ROM folder

---

## Your Folders, You Name Them!

Unlike other frontends, EasyArc doesn't force you to name your folders in any precise way. As long as your folder names loosely indicate the system — "My PS1 Games", "GameCube Collection", "GBA Stuff" — EasyArc figures out the rest automatically. No renaming. No reorganizing. Just point and play.

And when it's time to build your collection, creating a new folder is only a button click away.

## Supported Systems

| System | Emulator |
|--------|----------|
| GBA, GBC, N64, PS1, Genesis, NES, PSP, Atari 2600 | RetroArch |
| GameCube, Wii | Dolphin |

EasyArc currently supports **10 confirmed platforms** with more planned for future releases.

---

## Requirements

- macOS (Apple Silicon recommended — Windows support coming soon)
- [RetroArch](https://www.retroarch.com) installed
- [Dolphin](https://dolphin-emu.org) installed (for GameCube/Wii)
- Your own ROM files

---

## Box Art

EasyArc automatically downloads box art and game metadata for your library via [ScreenScraper](https://www.screenscraper.fr). A free ScreenScraper account is recommended for best results.
